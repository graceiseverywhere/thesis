Self-Storage City

As Americans consume more, their material goods are finding home in the self-storage industry. Bigger than ever, there is now over 1.7 billion square feet of rentable space, nearly the size of Washington D.C. 2017 was a record year in self-storage development (up almost 100 percent from the 26 million added in 2016).
