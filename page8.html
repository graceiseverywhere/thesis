And why are we storing our stuff away?

The range of reasons why people use self-storage correlate to meaningful parts of everyone’s life: family death to a growing family. The top reason being that people don’t have enough space at home. This suggests that Americans are consuming more stuff than they have space for and/or our living spaces have shrunken.
