Self-Storage Nation

Catering to America’s material overflow, the self-storage industry has exploded into a $36 billion dollar market. Nearly 1 out of 10 American households rent a self-storage unit and that number continues to grow. Visualizing survey data, this project examines how customers participate in this industry and how self-storage is a window into America’s relationship to consumption.
